Magic Cake

Magic Cake is one of the tastiest... LEGAL STUFF!!!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter

Directions

  1. Mix the eggs, sugar, and butter in a large bowl.
  2. Spread into a non-stick dish.
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
  4. Let sit at room temperature for 20 minutes.
  5. Eat and enjoy!
